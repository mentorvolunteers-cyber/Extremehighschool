Clubs & co-curricular life

Every student takes part in at least one club. Here are the ones the school is best known for, alongside the wider list students can join.

School choir

Rehearses several times a week and performs at venues across Uganda, with past invitations to perform in Poland.

Farming club

Grows crops for the dining hall and for study, easing food costs and giving biology and agriculture lessons a real plot of land.

Volunteer & exchange

Runs partnerships with schools in Europe and the US, giving students a route into international exchange programmes.

Debate & public speaking

Weekly practice debates build confidence and argument structure ahead of inter-school competitions.

Athletics & football

Track, field and football sessions run after classes, feeding into district and regional school competitions.

Drama & arts

Termly performances and craft projects give students outside academics a stage of their own.
