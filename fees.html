Requirements list

A separate list covers uniform, mattress, bedding and personal scholastic materials students bring at the start of term.

Payment schedule

Fees are typically payable at the start of term, with instalment arrangements available on request through the bursar's office.

Sponsorship

A limited number of sponsored places exist for students whose families cannot cover the full termly fee. Contact the office to ask.
